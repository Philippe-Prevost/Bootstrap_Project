MENU

Our tasting menu is £300. It is served for dinner from Monday to Friday, as well as for lunch on Fridays. We offer a shorter lunch menu for £180 on Wednesdays and Thursdays.

Our plentiful menu offers traditional and contemporary homemade pasta dishes - still to this day, made in-house by the family, as well as pizza, fresh seafood, meat and fish. Finish off with our divine homemade desserts and a delightful selection of wines from Italy and beyond.
